Want accessible tech education for everyone?

BridgeTheGapSG is a ground-up initiative to make tech education accessible to low-income families. Join us to build a world where youths are empowered to pursue tech regardless of their roots.

Interested? Get involved as a Mentor or sign up to be a Student

The Problem

In a world where digital transformation is the norm, programming will build the future of the world we live in today. However, not all students have equal opportunity to quality tech education.

BridgeTheGap wants to solve that.

Find out more. (link to problem statement)

Our mission

To be the bridge that allows disadvantaged youths to have access to tech education and to inspire them to explore tech as a possible career path.

Learn more

Our work

We pairs students with mentors on a 1-1 basis. Over the course of 3 months (12 weeks), mentors give an hour each week to meet with their mentee.
